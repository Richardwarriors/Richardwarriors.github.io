Biography

I am a second-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). My work focuses on advanced recommender systems, including LLM-based and multimodal RecSys, and user privacy in industrial RecSys under the guidance of Prof. Keke Chen.

I am also the photographer and co-founder of 3H-Studio in China, and I have lived and studied in the UK, China, and the US.

I collaborate with researchers from USTC, Duke University, USC, and the University of Cincinnati. If you are interested in collaboration, feel free to reach out.
