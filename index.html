Biography

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), working on developing advanced Recommender Systems (RecSys), including LLM-based and multimodal RecSys, as well as user privacy problems in industrial RecSys under the guidance of Prof. Keke Chen.

Before joining UMBC, I worked as a researcher in the Computer Science Department at Marquette University and served as a data scientist at HireBeat Inc..
